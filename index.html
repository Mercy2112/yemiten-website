About Yemiten Recipe Tube

Bringing authentic Ethiopian flavors and international cuisine to kitchens worldwide

Yemiten Recipe Tube has grown into one of the most trusted culinary education channels on YouTube. With over 200,000 dedicated subscribers and millions of views, we've built a global community of food enthusiasts.

Our channel specializes in authentic Ethiopian cuisine while exploring international flavors. Each video combines traditional cooking methods with modern techniques, ensuring you learn not just recipes, but the cultural stories behind every dish.

Beyond YouTube, we offer live cooking classes, brand partnerships, and culinary consulting services. Our mission is to preserve culinary traditions while making them accessible to home cooks everywhere.
